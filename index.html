How To Use The Speaker Cleaner

Follow these simple steps to clean your speaker effectively and safely:

1

Choose Your Mode

Select either Sound or Vibration mode depending on your device capability and personal preference. Sound mode uses specific frequencies to push water out, while Vibration uses your device's vibration motor.

2

Position Your Device

Place your device with the speaker facing downward to allow water or dust to be expelled by gravity. A slight angle often works best.

3

Start The Cleaner

Press the PRESS button to start the cleaning process. The process will run for 45 seconds using various frequencies designed to dislodge water and particles.

4

Repeat If Necessary

For heavily water-damaged speakers, you may need to run the process multiple times. Alternate between Sound and Vibration modes for best results.
